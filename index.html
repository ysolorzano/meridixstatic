Who we are.

We are a streaming sports platform, for everyone.

Easily live stream your own sports from anywhere, complete with HD video, audio, scores, and stats. Perfect for covering sports at any level, including local, high school, college, pro, and everything in between.

What we provide.

The Meridix platform tells your whole story with easy,
powerful, broadcasting features.

  • Easily live stream HD video

    Stream high quality video using off-the-shelf cameras or equipment you already have.

  • Track scores and stats in real time

    Utilize engaging, sport-specific player interfaces with scores and stats, and fan interaction.

  • Broadcast from a mobile device

    No computer? Broadcast from a smartphone or tablet and take webcasting anywhere.

  • Built for a novice or a pro

    Meridix fits whether you're an individual, a small team, or a major media company.

  • Everything is archived

    Every game video and audio is archived for later viewing, along with stats and other event data.

  • Reports to know your audience

    Understand your audience with statistics and deep analytics, including demographics.

  • Unlimited viewers and listeners

    Premium plans include unlimited viewers/listeners, and an unlimited number of events.

  • Audio-only live streaming option

    We offer audio-only options for broadcasters and radio stations who may not need to run video.

  • Record the game and upload later

    Lacking internet? Record the game now and upload it later. It will store just like a live game.

Features that help you make money.

We do more than just broadcast games.

We provide effective advertising tools

Premium accounts can monitize with advertising tools including pre-roll ads, banner ads, and on-air commercials. We even offer integration with third-party ad networks.

There are ticket and season pass selling options

Our end-to-end solution handles the payment process when you sell game tickets or season passes. Instantly accept credit cards and redeem your earnings via ACH transfer.

Start collecting earnings on instant downloads

Broadcasters can set custom prices on a game by game basis and sell copies of games for instant download, while fans get access to purchased files immediately.

Ad inventory is 100% yours

Sponsors, contributors, and boosters are there to support you. As a result, premium plans provide you with 100% of the banner or on-air ad space for your own use.
